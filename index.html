Structure of the HTML template

DOCTYPE

DOCTYPE is the beginner of html Structure.

HTML

HTML defines the meaning and structure of webpage content which consists of the opening and closing tag .

HEAD

The head is the top part of html who is holding all the information of webpage.

BODY

It displays the content of a website element containing all the contents of a HTML document, such as headings, paragraphs, images, hyperlinks, tables, lists, etc.
